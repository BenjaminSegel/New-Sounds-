This week, a new podcast, drawing from the hours of recorded audio by the two men Paul McCartney and Paul Muldoon. McCartney: A Life in Lyrics will feature 24 episodes across two seasons, each focusing on a different song. Among the tracks tackled in the first season will be “Eleanor Rigby,” “Back in the U.S.S.R.,” and “Live and Let Die.”
